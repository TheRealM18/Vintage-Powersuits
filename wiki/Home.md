# Power Armor Wiki

Electric power armor for **Vintage Story 1.20+**, built as an add-on to **Vintage Engineering**. Inspired by Machine Muse Powersuits: a "core" chestplate, energy storage on every piece, and toggleable modules.

- [Modules](Modules.md) — the upgrade system and full module list
